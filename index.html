About

I'm the eldest daughter of Vietnamese refugees, raised in Melbourne's west. That background — community, resourcefulness, showing up for each other — has shaped everything I do professionally.

For over two decades I've been working across sectors that don't usually talk to each other: local government, parliament, unions, training organisations, election campaigns, and the arts. I know how each world works, and I can move between them — which makes me useful in ways that are hard to replicate.

My career has been intentionally broad. Whether I'm designing a facilitated workshop for 200 people, emceeing a bilingual community event, helping an organisation figure out its strategy, or graphic recording a board meeting — the throughline is the same: I help people get clear, get aligned, and get moving.

I'm a single mum to Grace (15) and Teddy (12). I live in Sunshine North. I speak Vietnamese and English fluently. I make things — with words, with markers, with watercolour, with rooms full of people.

What I do

01

Workshop Facilitation & Design

Bespoke workshops on community organising, leadership development, power mapping, facilitation skills, public narrative, and consensus decision-making. In-person, online, or hybrid.

02

Campaign & Organisational Strategy

Co-authoring org strategies, theory of change, campaign design and evaluation. Done this for Democracy in Colour, the Green Institute, election campaigns, and grassroots collectives.

03

Emcee — Bilingual (EN/VI)

Confident emcee for events intimate or enormous — from a kitchen table of six to a public forum of thousands. Bilingual in English and Vietnamese. I'll say what is worth saying, for this moment and this gathering.

04

Graphic Recording

Live visual capture of meetings, workshops, and events. I translate what's said in the room into something people can see, share, and remember.

05

Illustration & Art Sessions

Watercolour (Australian florals), pen and ink illustration, and group or individual art sessions — all materials supplied. I'll come to you, or we can meet at one of my favourite cafes in the west.

06

Community Engagement & Strategy

Strategic advice on community engagement, volunteer systems, campaign structure, and organisational capacity — for nonprofits, advocacy orgs, and community groups at any stage.
